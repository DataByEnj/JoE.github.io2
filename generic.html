About Me

Welcome! I’m a data enthusiast driven by curiosity and a passion for uncovering insights through data. My journey began with an interest in cleaning and organizing raw data in Excel, which quickly evolved into turning that data into actionable stories.

In my experience across the nonprofit and higher education sectors, working in Excel has helped me build a solid foundation and grow in mastering techniques. From data cleaning and organization to leveraging functions, I have developed an understanding of efficiently manipulating and analyzing data in Excel.

This passion for data transformation led me to explore and deepen my knowledge of SQL and Tableau, focusing on cleaning, managing, and analyzing large datasets. In my professional pursuits, I have developed analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills and consistently use data to help teams report to stakeholders and meet goals. These tools revealed my passion for data management.

As I continue to grow my Excel, SQL, and Tableau skills, I am committed to using data to make an impact. I’m excited about using my skills to uncover insights that guide decisions and create growth opportunities for organizations.
